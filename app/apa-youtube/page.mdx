# APA 引用 YouTube 指南

<AnswerCard title="TL;DR" tldr="This page provides an overview of citing YouTube videos in APA7 format. It explains the general format for in‑text citations and reference entries, and links to detailed articles on special cases like missing authors or dates, Shorts and livestreams." />

YouTube is a rich source of information.  When you refer to a video in
an academic paper you must cite it properly.  The American
Psychological Association (APA) 7th edition provides guidelines for
citing online videos.  In general you need three pieces of
information: the author or channel, the date of upload, and the
video title.  In a reference list you write the title in sentence
case and identify the medium in square brackets.

<DataBox title="Example citation">
  <p><strong>In‑text citation:</strong> (CrashCourse, 2015)</p>
  <p><strong>Reference:</strong> CrashCourse. (2015). The French Revolution [Video]. YouTube. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lTTvKwCylFY</p>
</DataBox>
